ANG KWENTO (THE STORY)

THE MODERN PINOY is a tribute to Filipino heritage, built on a foundation of classic, comforting recipes passed down through generations. Our mission is to present these timeless flavors in a clean, modern, and uncompromising setting.

We blend the old-world heart of Filipino *pamilya* (family) cooking with a distinct brutalist aesthetic—a nod to raw, authentic design—reflecting the true, unfiltered taste of the Philippines.
